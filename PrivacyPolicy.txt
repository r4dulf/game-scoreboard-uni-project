# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 31, 2025_

This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and protected in the Game Scoreboard application.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Username**: Required for account registration.
- **Password**: Encrypted and stored securely.
- **Scores**: Game-related performance data.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to operate the application.

## 2. Use of Information

We use the collected information solely to:

- Authenticate users
- Track and update player scores
- Display leaderboard rankings

## 3. Cookies

We use cookies to:

- Manage session-based authentication (JWT in storage)
- Display cookie consent banner (required by GDPR)

You may control cookies through your browser settings.

## 4. Data Security

All user data is stored securely in a local SQLite database. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard methods.

## 5. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the functionality of the app.

## 6. User Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access your data
- Request deletion or correction
- Withdraw consent at any time
